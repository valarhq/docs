ValarAI is an inference platform purpose-built for agents that run for extended periods. We optimize for throughput at scale rather than low latency, serving models across large fleets.

Today, ValarAI exposes the [OpenAI Responses API](https://developers.openai.com/api/reference/resources/responses/methods/create) at `/v1/responses`.
