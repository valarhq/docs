## How billing works

You pay per token. Three rates apply to each request: **input** for the tokens you send, **cached** for input tokens served from a prefix cache, and **output** for the tokens the model generates. Every figure in the table below is in **USD per 1M tokens**.

Caching is automatic — ValarAI matches shared prompt prefixes for you and charges the lower cached rate on the tokens that hit. You can raise your hit rate by passing [`prompt_cache_key`](/api-reference/responses-api/create-a-response#body-prompt-cache-key) as a routing hint, but it's optional.

The rate you pay also depends on the completion window you request. ValarAI runs four windows — `asap`, `priority`, `standard`, and `flex` — and faster scheduling carries a higher rate. The table lists every model with its windows priced out; not every model offers every window. [Completion Windows](/completion-windows) explains the trade-offs, and you can mix windows per request.

<Note>
  Window coverage differs by model, and we keep adding models and widening window support. If a model or window you want isn't shown, <a href="mailto:support@valarhq.ai">get in touch</a>.
</Note>
